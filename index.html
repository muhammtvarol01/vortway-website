THE VORTWAY STANDARD

Three convictions that guide every shipment we touch.

Carrier Network That Already Runs Europe

Our routing draws on a vetted carrier pool spanning 50+ European corridors — built for redundancy, not promises. Every lane has a primary and a backup, every transit is tracked end-to-end, and every quote reflects real capacity, not aspirational pricing.

Trust-Driven Strategic Partnerships

Every partnership starts with a conversation, not a contract. We believe in earning your trust through consistent performance, transparent pricing, and honest communication. Your success is our reputation.

Unwavering Service Dedication

Your cargo is never just a shipment number to us. We understand the commercial value behind every load and the deadlines that matter to your business. Reliability isn't a promise — it's our standard.

FOUR FIXED COMMITMENTS

Verified, not aspirational.

EU Carrier Liability

Every shipment runs under the CMR Convention — international cargo liability is statutory, not optional.

GDPR-Compliant Data Handling

Shipper and consignee data is processed under EU GDPR Article 6(1)(b) — never resold, never repurposed.

Standard Trading Conditions

Our STC is published, fixed, and referenced on every quote — no surprise clauses, no hidden carve-outs.

Per-Shipment Cargo Insurance

Top-up policies available per load via our partner underwriter — full declared-value coverage when CMR limits aren't enough.
