WHAT WE MOVE, AND HOW

Four disciplines. Zero guesswork.

Full Truckload (FTL)

One shipment. One truck. One route.

  • Dedicated 13.6m trailer — your load never shares space
  • Direct door-to-door across all EU corridors
  • Named driver, single point of contact throughout

Groupage / LTL

You shouldn't pay for space you don't need.

  • Fixed weekly departures on Baltic → Central EU lanes
  • Transparent per-pallet pricing, no hidden terminal fees
  • From one pallet to half a truck — your choice, your cost

Customs Clearance

The border is where most problems start. Ours end there.

  • EU import, export, and T1 transit declarations handled in-house
  • Medininkai, Terespol, and Schengen entry points covered
  • Documentation prepared before the truck arrives — no waiting

Intermodal

Sometimes the most direct road has rails in it.

  • Road + rail combinations on long-haul EU corridors
  • Lower cost per km beyond 1,000 km, without sacrificing schedule
  • Containerized and palletized cargo both accepted
