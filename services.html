From full truckload moves to complex customs brokerage, every Vortway service is built around one principle: your cargo arrives on time, fully documented, and without surprises. Below is a detailed breakdown of what we do and how.

FULL TRUCKLOAD

FTL · Dedicated Capacity

Dedicated truck capacity for full loads — your cargo occupies the entire vehicle from origin to destination with no intermediate stops or co-loading. Ideal for time-sensitive, high-value, or large-volume shipments where speed and security are non-negotiable.

Key Benefits

  • Direct routing — no transhipment
  • Faster transit vs. LTL on same corridors
  • Full cargo security and accountability
  • Temperature-controlled options (ATP)
  • ADR-certified vehicles available
  • GPS tracking on all vehicles

Required Documents

  • CMR consignment note
  • Commercial invoice
  • Packing list
  • EUR.1 / T1 transit declaration (if applicable)
  • ADR transport document (hazmat only)
  • ATP certificate (temperature cargo)

LESS THAN TRUCKLOAD

LTL · Consolidated Freight

Cost-effective consolidated freight for partial loads. Your shipment shares vehicle capacity with other non-competing cargo, allowing you to pay only for the space you need while maintaining reliable transit times across our European network.

Key Benefits

  • Lower cost per unit vs. FTL for partial loads
  • Regular scheduled departures on key corridors
  • Suitable for 100kg – 6,000kg shipments
  • Pallet and non-palletised options
  • Cross-dock consolidation in Kaunas hub
  • Digital proof of delivery (ePOD)

Required Documents

  • CMR consignment note
  • Commercial invoice + packing list
  • Cargo dimensions and weight declaration
  • EUR.1 certificate (third-country goods)
  • Dangerous goods declaration (if applicable)

INTERMODAL TRANSPORT

Road · Rail · Sea · Combined

Combined transport solutions that leverage road, rail, and sea to optimise cost, transit time, and carbon footprint simultaneously. Particularly suited for high-volume, long-distance, or environmentally-focused logistics programmes where rail or sea legs offer material advantages.

Key Benefits

  • Up to 30% cost reduction on long-haul routes
  • Lower CO₂ vs. full road transport
  • Scalable for high-volume programmes
  • Rail hub access: Kaunas, Vilnius, Minsk, Warsaw
  • Port connections: Klaipėda, Gdańsk, Hamburg
  • Single point of contact for all modes

Required Documents

  • CMR (road leg) + CIM waybill (rail leg)
  • Bill of Lading (sea leg, if applicable)
  • Commercial invoice and packing list
  • Customs declarations for each border crossing
  • Intermodal transport unit (ITU) declaration

CUSTOMS BROKERAGE

EU Declarations · Compliance · Documentation

In-house customs expertise covering EU import and export declarations, tariff classification, duty optimisation, and full documentation management. We liaise directly with Lithuanian customs authorities and coordinate third-country procedures through our vetted partner network.

Key Benefits

  • Authorised Economic Operator (AEO) partnerships
  • HS tariff classification expertise
  • Duty drawback and preference scheme advice
  • T1 transit declaration management
  • ICS2 Entry Summary Declaration filing
  • Customs bond and guarantee arrangements

Required Documents

  • Commercial invoice with HS codes
  • Packing list with net/gross weights
  • Certificate of origin (EUR.1, Form A, or GSP)
  • Phytosanitary / veterinary certificates (if applicable)
  • EORI number of importer/exporter
  • Power of attorney for customs representation
